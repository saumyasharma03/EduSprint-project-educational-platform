Hackathons


1. Open Hackathon:

Open hackathons are general-purpose events where participants can work on projects of their choice. They have the freedom to innovate and create solutions in any field or industry they prefer.

2. Industry-Specific Hackathon

These hackathons are tailored to a particular industry or sector, such as healthcare, finance, or education. Participants tackle challenges and develop solutions relevant to that industry.



3. Hardware Hackathon:

Hardware hackathons involve creating physical devices or gadgets, often integrating software and electronics. Participants design and build prototypes with tangible, real-world applications.

4. Startup Hackathon:

In startup hackathons, participants work on business ideas, develop minimum viable products (MVPs), and pitch their startups to a panel of judges. These events often encourage entrepreneurship.



5.Codefest (Coding) Hackathon:


These hackathons focus primarily on coding challenges. Participants solve algorithmic problems, improve coding skills, and compete in coding competitions.
